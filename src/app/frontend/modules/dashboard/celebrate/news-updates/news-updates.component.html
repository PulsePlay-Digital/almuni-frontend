News Title:

Memorial Service for Prof. Dr. (Col) A. Balasubramanian, Founder Chancellor of Sri Balaji University, Pune (SBUP) on 14th March, 2021 at SBUP Campus

News Description:

Respected Sir/Madam,

1. It is with deep sorrow and regret, we inform you that our dearest, most respected and loved Bala Sir, left for his heavenly abode on 20 February 2021 and his funeral rites were held in the campus of Sri Balaji University, Pune on 21 February 2021.
2. Prof. Dr. (Col) A. Balasubramanian was an eminent educationist, philanthropist, a gifted writer and an illustrious army officer - A Legend in his lifetime. His legacy will be carried forward by the team he has created.
3. To pay homage to our beloved Bala Sir, a memorial service will be held on 14 March 2021 between 11:00 to 13:00 hrs, at Rathinathammal auditorium in Sri Balaji University, Pune. The entire memorial service will also be live streamed.
4. We understand that all of us in our hearts would like to be physically present for the memorial service but due to the pandemic situation it may not be possible for some. Therefore, there is an ption, where you may join live, for which the link is:
http://bit.ly/BalaSirMemorialService (Please note: This is an abbreviated link for Microsoft Team Link which will be live on 14 March 2021. To avoid last minute technical issues please download Microsoft Teams app on your device (mobile/desktop) in advance. Here is the link to download Microsoft Teams app https://www.microsoft.com/enin/microsoft-teams/download-app)
5. Keeping in line with the covid protocol and to also make the necessary arrangements, we request you to kindly fill up the RSVP form https://forms.gle/Xgj8ZDgTEJ1WU4Qy7 Using the same RSVP form, you may even send us your recorded tribute - written, audio or video which may be live streamed by us.
6. We cherish your association with our beloved Bala Sir and look forward to your continued relationship with Sri Balaji University, Pune.

Yours sincerely,
Mr. Parandhaman Balasubramanian.
