Continuous Learning

Late Prof. Dr. (Col.) A Balasubramanian was a voracious reader, himself and would always say one must read, learn, change, adapt to the changing times. Bala Sir would say – train your mind as you train you would train your body.

One of Bala Sir’s many visions was to empower students’ community through access to thousands of books, international magazines, newspapers from around India and the world, all available at the Sri Balaji University, Pune (SBUP) library.

One of Bala Sir’s dreams was to create a platform where students, alumni and corporate community would come together to learn and train even after the regular academic pursuits were over. Bala Sir believed one must constantly invest time and energy in learning, as it is a lifelong process.

Sri Balaji University, Pune has taken steps to implement it’s Honorable Founder President and Chancellor’s dream of ‘Continuous Learning’ through the below Learning & Development Initiatives that enable the learning journey of students, alumni, corporate leaders and entrepreneurs at a discounted exclusive costs to make it accessible and affordable to the wider audience.


SBUP Sri BUILD – MDP

Sri Balaji University, Pune presents the SBUP Digital Learning Platform whereby students, alumni, corporate executive, managers, leaders, SMB and entrepreneurs can sign-up online and continue their learning journeys from the comfort of their homes / offices at their own pace.

SBUP E-Learning Platform

Sri Balaji University, Pune presents the SBUP Digital Learning Platform whereby students, alumni, corporate executive, managers, leaders, SMB and entrepreneurs can sign-up online and continue their learning journeys from the comfort of their homes / offices at their own pace.

SBUP Alumni – Coursera Access

Sri Balaji University, Pune has signed an exclusive MoU with Coursera Platform to provide it’s students and alumni exclusive access to popular courses at discounted prices.

For SBUP current students – the Coursera training program are FREE.

For SBUP alumni exclusive – the Coursera training program access may be made available on a need basis. Please email alumni@sbup.edu.in
